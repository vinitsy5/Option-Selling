Calendar Spreads

Setup

  • SHORT: 3-7 DTE (fast decay)
  • LONG: 14-21 DTE (slow decay)
  • Strike: ATM (Delta ~0.50)
  • Type: PUT, CALL, or DOUBLE

Win Rate

TypeWin Rate
PUT Calendar55-60%
CALL Calendar55-60%
DOUBLE Calendar65-75% ⭐

Entry Rules

  • IV Rank/Percentile < 50
  • Range-bound market
  • ATM strike
  • Risk max 2-3% of account

Exit Rules

✅ Take profit: 50% gain
❌ Cut loss: -50% loss
🚫 Exit if: Price >7% away

Example

BTC ₹70,000
SHORT 3 DTE: ₹800
LONG 17 DTE: ₹1,800
Net Debit: ₹1,000

At expiry (BTC at ₹70k):
Short expires: +₹800
Long value: ₹1,400
Profit: ₹400 (40%!)
