Chapter 2: The Greeks

🔺 DELTA - Direction & Probability

Delta = Change in option price ÷ Change in underlying

Example: Delta 0.50 means if BTC moves ₹100, option moves ₹50
Also: Delta 0.50 = ~50% probability of expiring ITM

⏰ THETA - Time Decay

DTEDecay RateStrategy
60+ DTE~2%/dayBuy for calendars
7-15 DTE~10-15%/dayCalendar LONG leg
0-7 DTE~30-50%/dayCalendar SHORT leg!
🔥 Last 7 days = 30-50% of premium lost!

📊 VEGA - Volatility

Vega = Change per 1% IV change. Calendar spreads are LONG VEGA (profit from IV increase)

Strike Selection

🎯 RULE: Use ATM strike (Delta ~0.50) 95% of the time!

Why?
• Max time value = max theta
• Widest profit zone (±5-7%)
• Highest liquidity
• Best probability

Entry & Exit Rules

✅ ENTRY:
• IV Rank/Percentile < 50
• ATM strike
• SHORT: 3-7 DTE, LONG: 14-21 DTE
• Risk max 2-3% of account

🚪 EXIT:
• Take 50% profit (don't be greedy!)
• Cut at -50% loss
• Exit if price >7% away
• Close if IV spikes sharply

Real Example

💰 DOUBLE Calendar Trade:

Setup: BTC ₹70,000

Long still worth: ₹1,400

Profit Zone: ₹68,500 - ₹71,500 (±2.14%)
Win Probability: ~70%

Chapter 6: Advanced Topics

Position Sizing

Position Size = (Risk % × Account) ÷ Max Loss per Contract
Example:
Account: ₹10,00,000
Risk: 2%
Max loss: ₹500
Position = (0.02 × 10,00,000) ÷ 500 = 40 contracts
